**1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?**

**Personal information you disclose to us**

**In Short:** We collect personal information that you allow us to access through Spotify when you use our Services.

When you authenticate Harmoniq with your Spotify account, you grant us access to certain data from your Spotify account based on the permissions (scopes) you approve. The personal information we collect may include:

-   **Spotify User ID:** Your unique Spotify identifier.
-   **Playback Information:** Your current playback state, including the currently playing track.
-   **Playlists:** Information about your public, private, and collaborative playlists.
-   **Followed Artists:** The list of artists you follow on Spotify.
-   **Liked Songs and Library:** Your saved tracks and albums.
-   **Playback Control Permissions:** Ability to control playback on your devices.
-   **Premium Status:** Information about your subscription level (e.g., Free or Premium).

We do not process sensitive information such as your financial data or social security numbers.

**Application Data**

If you use our application, we may also collect the following information:

-   **Device Information:** Such as your mobile device ID, model, manufacturer, operating system, and version.
-   **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with our app, including the features you use and actions you take.
-   **Crash Data:** We use Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability and performance.

**Bug Reporting**

If you choose to submit a bug report via Google Forms, we collect the information you provide. We do not require you to submit your email address in the bug report.

**All personal information you provide must be true, complete, and accurate. Please notify us of any changes to such personal information.**

**Information automatically collected**

**In Short:** Some information is collected automatically when you use our Services.

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app, which does not reveal your specific identity but may include:

-   **Log and Usage Data:** Service-related, diagnostic, and performance information.
-   **Device Data:** Information about your device, such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.
